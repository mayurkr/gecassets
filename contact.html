HOW TO REACH VENUE: Road Travellers: Ponda is connected to the state capital, Panaji (28 km) by the National Highway 4A. The NH 4A also connects Ponda to the neighboring state of Karnataka. Buses run regularly between Panaji and Margao via Ponda. Rail/Train Travellers: The Margao (17 km) railway station is the nearest railhead to Ponda. One can hire a cab from there or take a bus. Air Travellers: The Dabolim airport (43 km) is the nearest airport to Ponda. One can hire a cab from there to GEC.

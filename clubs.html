Clubs

Robotics Club

Robotics club of GEC is a fraternity not just a group but a fraction of such students who are passionate about robotics. Meetings are held every week and various hardware and software techniques of different types of robots are discussed.We participate in various competitions and help the beginners to enhance their concepts related to robotics.

The Present President of the Robotics Club is Voith Mascarenhas

Photography Club

The Photography Club Of GEC is an initiative to inculcate an interest in photography among students of the college and to bring like minded individuals together. The club organises many events and photography related discussions within the college and aims to take photography in GEC to newer heights. It has been a active member in the organisation of PhotoFlare, a international Photography event.

The Photography Club is presently led by Akshay Parvatkar.
